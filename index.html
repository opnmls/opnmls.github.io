Why OpenMLS?

OpenMLS is revolutionizing the real estate industry by providing a free, open-source alternative to traditional Multiple Listing Services. Our platform aims to democratize access to property data, foster innovation, and create a more transparent real estate market.

Key Features:

  • Customizable listing input forms
  • Advanced search capabilities
  • Property history tracking
  • Integration with mapping services
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Data export in multiple formats
  • User roles and permissions management
