About OpenMLS

OpenMLS is a groundbreaking initiative to create an open-source Multiple Listing Service (MLS) platform. Our mission is to democratize access to real estate data, foster innovation in the industry, and create a more transparent and efficient property market.

Our Vision

We envision a world where real estate data is freely accessible, easily shareable, and not controlled by any single entity. By creating an open-source MLS, we aim to:

  • Reduce barriers to entry for new real estate technology startups
  • Improve data accuracy and consistency across the industry
  • Enable more advanced analytics and insights for property professionals and consumers
  • Foster collaboration between real estate professionals, developers, and data scientists

Our Story

2020

OpenMLS project conceived by a group of real estate tech enthusiasts and open-source advocates.

2021

Initial codebase developed and released on GitHub. First contributors join the project.

2022

OpenMLS gains traction. First pilot implementation launched with a local real estate board.

2023

Major version 1.0 released. OpenMLS begins to be adopted by multiple real estate associations and tech companies.
